Rochester Math Club

A nonprofit organization offering weekly meetings, competitions, and events for students in grades 1–12. They host the annual Rochester Math Festival and a summer camp at St. Olaf College. Weekly sessions run from October through May.

Visit them!

University of Minnesota Talented Youth Mathematics Program

A rigorous, accelerated program for advanced math students, offering high school and college-level coursework. Classes are held in Rochester and other Minnesota cities.

Main Website Entrance Exam Info

Math Masters

A competition for 4th–6th graders focusing on critical thinking and problem-solving. Students compete individually and in teams. Events are hosted in Rochester and other Minnesota locations.

Competition Website

Living Room Tutors

A free tutoring program through the Rochester Public Library that connects K–12 students with volunteers for help in math and other subjects.

Mathnasium of Rochester

Provides personalized tutoring for students in elementary through high school, both online and in-person. Focused on building confidence and mastery in math.

Private Tutoring Options

For 1-on-1 help, you can find local tutors via websites like Wyzant and Varsity Tutors. Both offer in-person and online math tutoring in the Rochester area.
