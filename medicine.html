Mayo Clinic Career Immersion Program

A summer program for Minnesota high school juniors and seniors to explore healthcare careers through hands-on activities at Mayo Clinic. Includes lab visits, career talks, and mentoring.

RISE for Youth Program (Mayo Clinic + NAACP)

A four-week summer program for high school juniors/seniors and college students, emphasizing health careers and leadership development for underrepresented youth.

Health Career Collaborative – Rochester

Offered through Mayo Clinic and Rochester STEM Academy, this mentoring initiative introduces high school students to medical careers and supports college readiness.

University of Minnesota Rochester STEAM Camp

A week-long summer camp for students entering grades 5–8 to explore STEAM concepts with hands-on science and medical-themed challenges in a college setting.
