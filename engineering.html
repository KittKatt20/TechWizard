UMR Summer STEAM Camp

A week-long camp for grades 5–8 offered by University of Minnesota Rochester. Focuses on hands-on science, technology, engineering, art, and math learning experiences.

Tech Academy STEM Camps

STEM-focused camps for ages 5–14, held at the Northrup Community Center through Rochester Community Education. Topics include robotics, coding, and engineering.

SPARK Camp

Exciting STEAM experiences that let students dive into engineering, science, and creativity with fun, hands-on activities. Camps fill quickly, so register early.

Rochester Regional STEM Fair

Annual event for students in grades 6–12 to present science and engineering projects, with potential to advance to state and international competitions.

RCTC Engineering & Physics Club

Rochester Community and Technical College offers a student club exploring engineering careers, with activities like tours, demos, and STEAM event participation.

Club Info

FIRST Robotics Team 2530 – Inconceivable

High school team (grades 9–12) competing in FIRST Robotics. Students design, build, and code robots to solve engineering challenges. Qualified for the World Championships in 2024 and 2025.

Team Website

CHS Robotics Summer Camp

A summer robotics camp hosted at Century High School for students in grades 6–9. Offers hands-on learning with guidance from FRC Team 2530 members.

Register Here

Tech Academy: Extreme Robotics – Battlebots

A robotics camp for grades 2–6 using LEGO® Mindstorms EV3. Teams build and program robots to battle in daily challenges like sumo wrestling and target practice.

Camp Info

Code Ninjas Robotics Program

Offers hands-on LEGO® SPIKE robotics programming for ages 7–14. Encourages critical thinking, problem-solving, and fun as kids build their own robotic creations.
